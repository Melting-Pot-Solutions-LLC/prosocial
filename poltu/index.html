Prosocial is an app that allows users to find nonprofits
and social businesses that support their local community.

Application Features

The prosocial application allows users, non-profit organizations and enterprises to interact with each other to achieve common social goals.

Users

Users select causes of interest and the organizations that fit their interests populates in a news feed, where they can easily choose a non-profit organization for donations. They can also purchase from businesses, a members of Prosocial, which offer discounts.
